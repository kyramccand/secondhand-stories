Secondhand Stories

Give Every Story a Second Chapter.
Secondhand Stories recycles books from users and closing libraries by purchasing them in mass or accepting donations, then reselling them at very low costs.
As libraries close and communities lose access to books, our goal as a company is to combat this by keeping books in circulation and ensuring reading remains accessible.

Why Choose Us?

We keep books out of landfills by sourcing them from closing libraries and community donations. This allows book to be given a second life and keeping stories in circulation.
We believe cost shouldn't be a barrier in access to reading or books. So, we offer our books at affordable prices so that everyone can discover and enjoy new stories.
We accept all sorts of donations. You can offer your old unused books or books you discovered through us. Then, you can earn credit towards your next read from Secondhand Stories.
